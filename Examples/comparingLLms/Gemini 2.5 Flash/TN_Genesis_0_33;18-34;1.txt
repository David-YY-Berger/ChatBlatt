Jacob arrived safe in the city of Shechem which is in the land of Canaan-having come thus from Paddan-aram-and he encamped before the city. The parcel of land where he pitched his tent he purchased from the kin of Hamor, Shechems father, for a hundred kesitah s., a unit of unknown value. He set up an altar there, and called it El-elohe-yisrael.Now Dinah, the daughter whom Leah had borne to Jacob, went out to visit the daughters of the land.
