ENGLISH:
About three months later, Judah was told, Your daughter-in-law Tamar has played the harlot; in fact, she is pregnant from harlotry. Bring her out, said Judah. She should be burned! As she was being brought out, she sent this message to her father-in-law, Its by the man. to whom these belong that Im pregnant. And she added, Examine these: whose seal and cord and staff are these? Judah recognized them, and said, She is more in the right than I, inasmuch as I did not give her to my son Shelah. And he was not intimate with her again.
