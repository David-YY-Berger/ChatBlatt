ENGLISH:
Please accept my present which has been brought to you, for God has favored me and I have plenty. And when he urged him, he accepted. And [Esau] said, Let us start on our journey, and I will proceed at your pace. But he said to him, My lord knows that the children are frail and that the flocks and herds, which are nursing, are a care to me; if they are driven hard a single day, all the flocks will die. Let my lord go on ahead of his servant, while I travel slowly, at the pace of the cattle before me and at the pace of the children, until I come to my lord in Seir. Then Esau said, Let me assign to you some of the people who are with me. But he said, Oh no, my lord is too kind to me!
