When the time came for her to give birth, there were twins in her womb! While she was in labor, one of them put out a hand, and the midwife tied a crimson thread on that hand, to signify: This one came out first. But just then it drew back its hand, and out came its brother; and she said, What a breach, connected with Perez. you have made for yourself! So he was named Perez. Afterward his brother came out, on whose hand was the crimson thread; he was named Zerah.
