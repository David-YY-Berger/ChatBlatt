ENGLISH:
Cain said to HASHEM, My punishment is too great to bear! Since You have banished me this day from the soil, and I must avoid Your presence and become a restless wanderer on earth-anyone who meets me may kill me! HASHEM said to him, I promise, if anyone kills Cain, sevenfold vengeance shall be exacted. And HASHEM put a mark on Cain, lest anyone who met him should kill him. Cain left the presence of HASHEM and settled in the land of Nod, east of Eden.
